ESMart.

Client background.

Esmart or its old name, Hajjah Yamah is a small grocery store based in Sijangkang, Selangor. It has been operating retail for almost 5 years now. This project is huge step for this business as they are going online where customers can order groceries on their brand new e-commerce website and the products are then delivered to their doorstep. This project has enabled me to utilize my UX/UI design knowledge and apply it on a website. Both the design and front end development is done by me. The backend development is done by my partner.

Application.

The client request both a landing page and an E-commerce website. The landing page is done from scratch, meanwhile the E-Commerce website is done in Wordpress due to its easy control dashboard and various business plugins to help our client.
