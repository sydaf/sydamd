Apdhl x Crime.

Client background.

Both APDHL and Crime are both local streetwear brand. Styling the streets of Telok Panglima Garang with their unique take on fashion.

Thinking process.

This project was my first introduction to doing t-shirt design and layout. The brand’s nature resonates skateboarding, grunge and brutalism. The inspiration for this design came from “Thrasher”.

Instead of the usual bright orange and red color usually used in this format, I used purple and blue to ease the eye from transtitioning between the black background to the white text.
